About

I'm Sean. Originally from Liverpool, I'm a fourth year Computer Science student at the University of York and I don't update this blog as often as I ought to!

I'm interested in programming, electronics and more. You can see examples of some things I've made on the projects page and on my GitHub profile.

I have a small collection of vintage computers, which you can see and read about in this post. I also have an affection for all things that fly under remote control, mostly quadcopters, and I like building and flying them in my spare time.

Besides that I also playing the guitar or occasionally piano. I like to read classic science fiction and fantasy as well as biographies and historical books. I also like walking, and I'm a committee member of and current webmaster for the University of York Outdoor Society. I'm also on the committee of DroneSoc.

If you want to contact me, please feel free to email me.
