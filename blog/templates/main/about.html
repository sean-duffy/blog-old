About

I'm Sean. I'm currently 18 years old and in my final year of sixth form in Liverpool, England. If all goes well I'll be studying a degree in Computer Science at the University of York in September this year.

I'm interested in programming and web development - at the moment I'm working mostly in Python and Java. You can see examples of some things I've made on the projects page, or at least you will be able to when I get around to adding to it.

I have a keen interest in the history of computers and I've amassed a small collection of vintage computers, which consists of a Commodore 64, TI-99/4A, BBC Micro and Commodore PET 3032 series. I've also recently developed an affection for all things that fly under remote control, mostly quadcopters, and I like tinkering with and flying those in my spare time.

Besides theat I also enjoy electronics, physics and playing the guitar or occasionally piano. I like to read classic science fiction as well as biographies and historical books.

You can also find me on Facebook, Tumblr, GitHub, SoundCloud and YouTube. If you want to contact me, please feel free to email me. If you'd like to hire me, here is my CV.
