Experience

Aquarium Software - Technical Support 2012

In the summer of 2012 I spent two weeks at Aquarium Software, a company which distributes a case and claims management software service called Aquarium. During the placement I had to quickly gain an understanding of the software and then use that knowledge to respond to support tickets from the clients of Aquarium, communicating effectively to solve the issues they were having.

I also spent some time with the developers of Aquarium, who gave me an introduction to the technologies behind the software and allowed me to write some code in C#, a language which at the time was completely new to me. This placement was an excellent opportunity for me to experience what it is like to work at a software company.

University of Liverpool - Research Assistant 2012

I participated in a work experience placement in the department of Electrical Engineering and Electronics at the University of Liverpool for a duration of two weeks in 2012. In this placement, I was assigned to work with a group conducting research into the development of a rectification device capable of operating in the terahertz frequency range.

During my time at this placement, I was able to gain skills such as the use of scientific optical and electrical measurement apparatus, as well as analysis of the data produced by these instruments.

Taskers PLC - IT Support 2010
